Mango Pomelo Sago Pudding
ingredeints:
1. 3 Mangos
2. 1/2 Pomelo ( optional )
3. 200 ml Coconut Milk/ canned full-fat coconut milk (for a richer flavor)
4. 300 ml milk
5. 150 ml Drinking Water
6. 60 g Suagr
7. 100 g sago / tapioca pearls
8. ice
1. Cook Sago/tapioca pearls:
In a pot with water, leave it to a boiling point. Once that is done, insert the sago/tapioca pearls into the pot. Cover the pot and stir it intermittently until it looks transpaerent. This should take for about 15 - 20 min.
Drain it and mix it with coconut milk and put it in the fridge
2. Mixing Ingredients:
In another pot, have the sugar, milk, coconut milk, and water (150 ml) mixed together. Bring it to a boil and stop right there. Place it into the fridge for fast cooling.
3. Make the Mango Pure√©:
Blend the mangoes, your mixed ingredeints, and the ice in a high-powdered blender smooth.
(Optional: You can add more sugar for more sweetness) 4. How to Assemble:
Place your blended mango into a glass.
Layer it with the mixed sago/tapioca pearls.
Top it off with some diced mangoes, pomelo segments. ( You may add more coconut milk if you wish )
